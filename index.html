Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

At SlyOS ("we", "our"), we believe the most secure data is data we never touch. Our architecture is designed to keep user prompts and model inference strictly on the end-user's device.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not—and technically cannot—access the content of the conversations (prompts and completions) generated on the device using the SlyOS SDK. All inference happens locally on the hardware (NPU/GPU/CPU).

2. Telemetry Data

To provide dashboard analytics to our customers (the Application Providers), the SDK sends minimal technical telemetry to our servers in AWS (us-east-2), including:

  • Device performance metrics (Latency, Token generation speed).
  • Hardware specifications (Device model, RAM, OS version).
  • Error logs related to model downloading or loading.

3. Infrastructure Security

Our backend is hosted on AWS Elastic Beanstalk with strictly configured VPCs. Database connections are encrypted via TLS. We do not sell or share telemetry data with third parties.
